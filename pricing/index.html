Questions about plans

What counts as a submission?

One completed application sent to one employer. Filling a form without sending it costs nothing, and neither does browsing the live database.

Do unused submissions roll over?

No. The free tier resets to 100 at the start of each month.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes. Pro is month to month. Your data and profile stay intact if you drop back to Free.
