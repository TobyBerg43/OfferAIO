Employer FAQ

What does it cost?

Nothing upfront. You pay a placement fee only when you hire a candidate we introduced. Fee structure is agreed before any introductions are made.

What does pre-screened mean?

Every candidate has a structured profile: resume, school, major, graduation year, work authorization, role preferences, and match scores against your role requirements. You see complete, consistent data instead of a resume pile.

How fast can we see candidates?

Most partners see a first candidate slate within two weeks of finalizing role requirements. Summer 2027 hiring timelines are supported through the full season.

How is candidate data handled?

Candidates opt in before their profile is shared with any employer. Until then, everything you see is anonymized.

Ready to build your intern pipeline?

The partner application takes ten minutes. We reply within two business days.
