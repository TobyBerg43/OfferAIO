Four steps, about five minutes. Then every internship application is one click.

1

Install the OfferAIO extension

OfferAIO runs as a Chrome extension so applications go out from your own browser, on your own logged-in sessions — never from our servers.

Almost there: the extension is in review with the Chrome Web Store. Drop your email and we'll send the install link the moment it's approved.
Get the install link → Download .zip (manual install)
manual install: chrome://extensions → enable Developer mode → Load unpacked
2

Pin it to your toolbar

Click the puzzle-piece icon in Chrome's toolbar, find OfferAIO, and hit the pin. You'll want it one click away on every application page.

takes 5 seconds
3

Add your profile once

Name, school, major, grad date, work authorization, LinkedIn, and an optional cover-letter template. Saved locally in your browser — it never touches our servers. Fill it in the extension popup, or set it up on your dashboard and it syncs across automatically.

Open the dashboard
about 10 minutes, once per season
4

Open any posting and hit Fill

On Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workday, Handshake, LinkedIn — or any employer link you paste — click Fill this application. OfferAIO completes the fields, drafts your cover letter in your voice, and highlights the resume box for you. You review, then submit.

Browse live Summer 2027 listings
1 click per application

What OfferAIO will never do

  • Submit without you — you approve every application (full-auto is opt-in and clearly labelled)
  • Bypass a CAPTCHA — if one appears, it's handed straight back to you
  • Upload your resume for you — browsers forbid it, so you attach the file yourself
  • Send your profile to our servers — it stays in your browser
